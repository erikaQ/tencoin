    Selection process

    We believe that the key to success is being yoyrself as it is what makes you unique. Of course, we also value professionalism and sincerity from your side.

    The selections process consists jf several steps

    • Apply for the job through our website by sending your CV and cover letter

    • We will invite successful candidate for the initial interview with our HR manager
